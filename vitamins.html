Vitamins made simple

Vitamins help your body stay healthy. They support your eyes, skin, bones, and energy.

Why vitamins matter

Your body needs vitamins in small amounts every day. They come from food, sunlight, and sometimes supplements.

Common vitamins

Vitamin A

Good for vision and skin.

Vitamin C

Helps protect your body and heal wounds.

Vitamin D

Helps build strong bones.

Vitamin E

Supports healthy cells and skin.

Vitamin K

Helps with blood clotting.

B Vitamins

Help turn food into energy.

Easy tips

  • Eat fruits and vegetables every day.
  • Choose whole grains, beans, nuts, and eggs.
  • Get sunlight for vitamin D when possible.
