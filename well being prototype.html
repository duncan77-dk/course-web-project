Baby

Baby health focuses on growth, nutrition, sleep, hygiene, vaccinations, and helping babies develop safely during their first years of life. Babies need regular care and attention because early childhood strongly influences future health and development.

Baby care includes keeping your baby clean, changing diapers regularly, ensuring safe sleep habits, giving routine vaccinations, and monitoring milestones such as smiling, crawling, sitting, and walking.

What Babies Should Eat

  • Breast milk or infant formula provides important nutrients during early months.
  • From around 6 months, soft foods such as mashed fruits and vegetables can be introduced.
  • Healthy foods include bananas, avocado, sweet potatoes, carrots, oatmeal, and soft proteins.
  • Provide enough fluids and avoid foods that may cause choking.

Baby Care Tips

  • Ensure enough sleep.
  • Keep vaccinations updated.
  • Practice gentle play and interaction.
  • Regular healthcare checkups.
