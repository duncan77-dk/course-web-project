Supplements made simple

Supplements can help fill gaps in your diet, but they work best when used carefully and with good food choices.

What supplements are

Supplements are products that add nutrients or other ingredients to your routine. They may include vitamins, minerals, protein, or herbal blends.

Common supplements

Protein powder

Helps support muscle recovery and daily protein needs.

Vitamin D

Often used when someone needs extra support for bone health.

Fish oil

May support heart and brain health for some people.

Probiotics

Can support digestion and gut health.

Helpful tips

  • Choose supplements that match your needs.
  • Read labels and follow the recommended amount.
  • Talk to a healthcare professional before starting a new supplement.
